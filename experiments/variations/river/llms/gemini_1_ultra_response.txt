Steps:

Take the mouse across the river. Leave the snake and cheese on the LeftBank.
Return alone.
Take the snake across the river.
Bring the mouse back. Now the mouse is back on the LeftBank with the cheese.
Take the cheese across the river.
Return alone.
Take the mouse across the river.
Explanation:

It's important to prevent the mouse and cheese or the snake and mouse from being left alone together on either bank.
We need to use some back-and-forth movements to safely transport everything.
